Electric Fencing at Saferon

Electric fence systems form the first line of defence in modern perimeter security. By delivering a safe but powerful electric pulse, these systems deter intruders while instantly detecting and reporting fence interference.

At Saferon Systems Limited, we design and install high-quality electric fencing solutions that integrate seamlessly with alarm systems, CCTV, and access control platforms.

For Homes

  • Intruder Deterrence: Prevent unauthorized entry before it happens.
  • Early Warning: Instant alerts when the fence is touched or cut.
  • Safe & Legal: Designed to meet safety standards for residential use.

For Offices & Commercial Properties

  • Perimeter Protection: Secure office compounds and sensitive areas.
  • System Integration: Works with CCTV and intruder alarms.
  • Reduced Guarding Costs: Enhances security without heavy manpower.

For Industrial Facilities

  • High-Security Perimeters: Protect factories and warehouses.
  • 24/7 Monitoring: Detect tampering in real time.
  • Reliable Performance: Built for harsh environments.

For Institutions & Compounds

  • Controlled Boundaries: Secure schools, hospitals, and estates.
  • Clear Deterrence: Visible fencing discourages intrusion.
  • Scalable Design: Suitable for small and large perimeters.
