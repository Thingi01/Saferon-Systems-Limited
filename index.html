About Saferon Systems

Established in 1990, SAFERON SYSTEMS LIMITED stands as one of the longest serving electronic security and IT providers in Nairobi, Kenya, committed to delivering dependable and cutting-edge security and IT infrastructure services to both private and commercial sectors.

30+ Years of Excellence

Specializing in the Security and IT sectors, our skilled Engineers excel in implementing security and IT solutions for a diverse clientele including Commercial, Industrial, Diplomatic Missions, schools, private premises, and property management companies.
