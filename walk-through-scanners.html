Walkthrough Scanners at Saferon

Walkthrough scanner solutions are critical components of modern security systems, designed to detect concealed items and ensure safety across a wide range of facilities.

These systems provide a fast, non-invasive method for screening individuals, making them ideal for offices, buildings, malls, airports, hotels, and embassies.

For Offices

  • Enhanced Security: Detect concealed weapons or prohibited items.
  • Non-Intrusive Screening: No physical contact required.
  • Compliance: Helps meet workplace security standards.

For Buildings

  • Visitor Screening: Control access at entry points.
  • Threat Detection: Rapid identification of risks.
  • Space Efficient: Easy to integrate into entrances.

For Malls

  • Crowd Management: High-throughput screening.
  • Theft Prevention: Detect prohibited items early.
  • Integrated Security: Works with CCTV systems.

For Airports

  • Passenger Screening: Aviation-grade security checks.
  • Operational Efficiency: Handles large volumes quickly.
  • System Integration: Complements ID & baggage screening.

For Hotels

  • Guest Safety: Screen entry discreetly.
  • Efficient Access: Seamless integration with check-in.
  • Staff Protection: Prevents concealed threats.

For Embassies

  • High-Security Screening: Detect concealed risks.
  • Controlled Access: Secure sensitive areas.
  • Comprehensive Protection: Integrates with CCTV & access control.
